======================================
Developing Plugins for Paper Platforms
======================================

Hey, developer. The pages within this section are written with the hope of providing developers with interest in creating plugins for Paper platforms a valuable source of tips, tricks, and guides.

If you want to add new commands and mechanics or listen and respond to events happening on the Paper server or Waterfall proxy in real time, then you are in the right place. Developing a Plugin for Paper or Waterfall will allow you to do these things.

If you are not yet familiar with Paper or Waterfall, see their respective documentations: :doc:`../server/index` and :doc:`../waterfall/index`.
